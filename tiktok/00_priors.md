Commit this file **before** any sampling, scraping, or competitor analysis. Its purpose is to make confirmation bias visible later: when findings are settled, compare what you actually believed up front to what the data showed. Vague predictions defeat the point — be specific enough that you could be wrong.

## Predictions

P1. **Underserved sub-niche.** The underserved sub-niche in PT-BR astrology TikTok is __________ because __________.

P2. **Winning format.** The best-performing video format will be __________ (talking head / text-on-screen / b-roll / mixed) because __________.

P3. **Optimal length.** The best video length range is __________ seconds because __________.

P4. **Hook style.** The hook type with highest 3-second retention will be __________ (question / hot-take / claim / story / list / demo / pattern-interrupt) because __________.

P5. **Cadence and timing.** Optimal cadence is __________ videos per week, posted at __________ (BRT) because __________.

P6. **Audience age band.** The most reachable audience is __________ years old.

P7. **Tone register.** The audience will respond best to a tone that is __________ (mystical / academic / casual / humorous / authoritative / vulnerable).

P8. **Regional signal.** Regional accent or cultural cues from the __________ region will outperform unmarked / neutral delivery — or unmarked will win, if that's your prediction.

P9. **First viable monetization.** The realistic first revenue stream within 6 months will be __________ (1:1 readings / digital products / courses / brand deals / affiliate / none yet).
